1. What This Is

The Genesis Chamber is a standalone tool that runs multi-persona AI debates for creative projects. Think of it as a virtual creative agency where 5-13 AI participants â€” each loaded with a deep consciousness profile of a legendary creative figure â€” debate, critique, and refine ideas across multiple rounds until a winning concept emerges.

Core Idea: Instead of asking one AI "give me a marketing concept," you ask 5 AI personas of legendary marketers to independently create concepts, anonymously critique each other, get directed by an AI Steve Jobs, refine, and repeat for 6+ rounds. The output quality is dramatically higher.

5. Five-Stage Round System

STAGE 1 â€” CREATE
Independent Concept Generation
Each participant works alone. Soul document fully loaded. Generates 1-3 concepts with name, idea, headline, visual direction, copy, rationale, image prompt, video prompt. No access to others' work.
STAGE 2 â€” CRITIQUE
Anonymized Peer Review
All concepts shuffled and labeled "Concept A, B, C..." â€” nobody knows whose is whose. Each participant scores and critiques EVERY concept (including their own, unknowingly). Prevents ego-protection. Creates honest feedback.
STAGE 3 â€” SYNTHESIZE
Moderator Direction Setting
Steve Jobs (moderator) receives all concepts + all critiques + aggregate scores. Identifies consensus, dissent, hidden gems. Eliminates weak concepts. Sets direction and constraints for next round. Jony Ive (evaluator) adds craft assessment.
STAGE 4 â€” REFINE
Directed Evolution
Each participant revises based on critique + moderator direction. Can evolve, merge with another concept, pivot entirely, or defend unchanged. Eliminated participants join surviving concepts as collaborators.
STAGE 5 â€” PRESENT
Group Presentation
Each participant presents refined concept. Includes evolution notes (what changed and why). Moderator gives immediate 3-sentence reaction. Sets up next round.
