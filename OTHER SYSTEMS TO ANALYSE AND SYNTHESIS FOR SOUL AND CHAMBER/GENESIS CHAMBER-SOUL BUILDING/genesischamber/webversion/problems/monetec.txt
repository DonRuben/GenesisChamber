# MONETEC STRATEGIC BRIEFING & CONSULTANT QUESTIONS

## EXECUTIVE BRIEFING: MONETEC AT A GLANCE

### Company Overview
**Monetec GmbH** - Austrian company (Vienna, May 2024) tokenizing renewable energy investments
- **Founders**: Marat Perlin (CEO), Günther Gehring, Heiko Schieser
- **Heritage**: Partners with 10+ years experience, 170+ solar projects, €100M invested
- **Innovation**: First to tokenize solar assets - making Spanish solar accessible to German investors via blockchain

### The Product
- **MTC Tokens**: Digital securities representing profit participation rights
- **Underlying Assets**: Solar installations in Mallorca (starting with 350 kWp Cas Canar project)
- **Returns**: 6.7-8.6% p.a. based on actual electricity generation
- **Distribution**: 85% of profits to investors, 15% retained by Monetec
- **Minimum Investment**: €100,000
- **Payment**: Bank transfer or crypto (USDT)

### Market Opportunity
- **Mallorca Energy Gap**: Needs 7,000-8,500 new PV installations by 2030 (currently 85% fossil fuels)
- **German Investor Base**: 5M Germans visit Mallorca annually, 38% of property buyers are German
- **Tokenization Market**: Projected €18.9 trillion by 2033
- **Competitive Advantage**: First mover in tokenized renewable energy

### Current Status
- **2024 Performance**: €196,384 invested, 8.62% preliminary return
- **Infrastructure**: Investor dashboard live, KYC process established
- **Regulatory**: EU-compliant, Austrian FMA framework
- **Pipeline**: Multiple projects identified in Mallorca
